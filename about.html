  • About Natasha Lee

    Natasha is a small creative with big dreams of moving the world. Her optimistic aspirations are fueled by her love for communication and storytelling and are presented through her skills in illustration and brand strategy. The nature of her projects is bright and playful but carries messages of social change and education. With brand strategy, she works well with words and research, distilling client visions to their core values.
