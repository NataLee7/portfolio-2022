COMMIT

Meat Your Match Meat Eaters

Currently, offal is available at your local Whole Foods and niche supermarkets, but these packages are often hidden at the bottom of shelves and remain a gross mystery to most shoppers. To combat this issue of approachability and visibility, my packaging is purposefully eye catching and embraces the weirdness of the product. While I acknowledge that cardboard sleeves runs the risk of overpackaging, this was the key tactic for differentiating Commit's brand and movement from the crude saran wrapped products currently on shelves.

Commit's line of offal appeals to meat eaters in North America who are primarily looking for an exciting culinary conquest, and passively seeking ways to justify their dietary choices. Tapping into the push-back culture against veganism, the tone of the copy is brash and cheeky. Visual elements are clean and bold, reminiscent of familiar meat packaging trends.

In the end this project was all about challenging the status quo of what we consider part of a meat eater's selection.
